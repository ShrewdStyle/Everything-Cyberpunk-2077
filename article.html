What is Cyberpunk 2077?

Written by Jake Knight - Jan 01 2020
Entertainment

Cyberpunk 2077 is an upcoming RPG developed and published by CD Projekt Red, this is their next major IP after the Witcher 3 which has seen huge success since its launch in May 2015. Cyberpunk is set in the year 2077 and is set to be a look into the future where society is on the verge of collapse, the major corporations have taken over and are now in control of everything. You will play a character (V) who is a mercenary trying to work his/her way up the ranks of society.

One of the major appealing aspects of Cyberpunk 2077 is that you get to play the game how you want, there are multiple decisions to be made which will all have an impact on the overall story and how your character is shaped. Will you go down the stealth aspect and decide to hack your way into the system to find a quiet approach to tackling dangerous situations, or will you go guns blazing and dive head first into the problem, there are countless ways to play this game. Cyberpunk is at the point where AI is everywhere, where people can purchase replace body parts with machinery in order to improve/upgrade themselves, if or how you choose to upgrade your character is again entirely up to you... Upgrade too much though and you may start to see yourself lose control of your sanity and could have a run in with the Night citys law enforcement.
